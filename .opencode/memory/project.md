# Project: Heard Again

## Mission
To create a "Living Archive" for family history, enabling descendants to preserve, interact with, and experience the stories and voices of their ancestors through a digital medium.

## Overview
Heard Again is a contextualized digital platform designed to host family legacies. It focuses on audio-centric narratives, where the experience dynamically shifts based on the selected family member, providing a deep, immersive connection to individual histories.

## Key Tech Stack
- **Framework**: Next.js (Pages Router)
- **UI Library**: MUI (Material UI) with Emotion
- **Database**: PostgreSQL
- **ORM**: Prisma
- **Authentication**: NextAuth.js
- **Infrastructure**: Linux-based development, Tailscale for secure service networking
