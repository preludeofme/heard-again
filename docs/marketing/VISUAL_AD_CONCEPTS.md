# Heard Again — Visual / Ad Concepts

> **Tone:** Warm, nostalgic, modern — NOT corporate stock photos  
> **Brand Accents:** Purple #9C69AC, Teal #3F6271, Sage #8EC384  
> **Target Platforms:** Facebook, Pinterest, Instagram

---

## Concept 1: The Melody of Memory

**Scene:** An antique wooden music box sits open on a sunlit windowsill. Instead of a traditional ballerina, a shimmering golden voice waveform rises from the mechanism, curling upward like smoke. Tiny dust motes dance in the afternoon light around the waveform, and the box's interior lining is a faded velvet in the app's signature purple.

**Overlay text:** Your voice. Her story. Forever.

**Caption:** Some melodies never fade. Preserve the stories behind the voices you love most. ✨🎵

**Best platform:** Instagram
**Color palette:** Warm amber, antique gold, dusty purple, cream, soft sepia

---

## Concept 2: Roots That Sing

**Scene:** A hand-illustrated family tree rendered in warm brown ink on parchment paper. The trunk and branches twist gracefully, but each leaf is a tiny green audio waveform icon. Small golden dots at the branch junctions mark where different family members' stories begin. A watercolor wash of soft sage green and cream fills the background.

**Overlay text:** Where every branch has a voice.

**Caption:** Watch your family tree grow — one voice at a time. Every leaf is a story waiting to be heard. 🌿🎙️

**Best platform:** Pinterest
**Color palette:** Ink brown, sage green (#8EC384), cream parchment, muted gold, sepia

---

## Concept 3: Letters You Can Hear

**Scene:** A pair of vintage, worn-leather headphones rests across an aged handwritten letter, their cord trailing off the edge of a wooden desk. The letter is slightly yellowed and covered in elegant cursive writing. A single dried flower — a lavender sprig — lies beside the letter. Soft window light casts a warm glow, and a subtle purple hue tints the shadows.

**Overlay text:** Read their words. Hear their voice.

**Caption:** A letter tells you what they thought. Their voice tells you how they felt. 🖋️🎧

**Best platform:** Facebook
**Color palette:** Worn leather brown, parchment cream, lavender purple, warm shadow grey

---

## Concept 4: Stories at Sundown

**Scene:** A wide wooden porch swing hangs empty on a wraparound veranda during the golden hour. A cup of tea with a faint wisp of steam sits on the seat beside a small vintage microphone. Long shadows stretch across the wooden planks. The sky behind the porch is a gradient of amber to deep teal, with the silhouette of a large oak tree framing the scene.

**Overlay text:** Save a seat for their story.

**Caption:** The best conversations happen at dusk. Don't let theirs fade with the light. 🌅🎤

**Best platform:** Facebook
**Color palette:** Amber sunset, deep teal (#3F6271), warm wood brown, twilight purple, cream

---

## Concept 5: Recipes That Remember

**Scene:** A close-up of a rustic kitchen shelf holding mismatched glass spice jars with handwritten labels. One jar is pulled slightly forward, its label reading "Grandma's Apple Pie — listen to the story" in delicate script. A small QR-code-style soundwave icon is printed below the text. Behind the jars, a faded recipe card is tucked into a cookbook, and warm light spills from a nearby window.

**Overlay text:** Every recipe has a story. Taste it.

**Caption:** That secret ingredient? It was never the cinnamon. 🥧🎧

**Best platform:** Pinterest
**Color palette:** Warm terracotta, cream, sage green, amber, faded paper brown

## Concept 6: Constellations of Memory

**Scene:** A dark night sky viewed from above, as if lying on a hill looking up. But the stars are not random — they trace the shape of a human voice waveform, glowing in soft purple and teal against the deep indigo sky. A faint golden thread connects the dots like a constellation map. Silhouettes of pine trees line the bottom edge of the frame.

**Overlay text:** Their voice is written in the stars.

**Caption:** Some voices shine forever. Map your family's constellations of memory. ✨🌌

**Best platform:** Instagram
**Color palette:** Deep indigo, glowing purple (#9C69AC), teal (#3F6271), star gold, midnight blue

## Concept 7: Time Told in Stories

**Scene:** An open vintage pocket watch rests on a worn leather armchair. But instead of clock hands, a tiny golden audio waveform arcs between the 12 and 6 positions. The watch's inner casing is engraved with a single name and date. Outside the watch, the waveform expands and floats upward, fading into translucent golden threads. The background is a soft cream with warm amber lighting.

**Overlay text:** Time stops. Their voice doesn't.

**Caption:** A pocket watch holds time. Heard Again holds what time can't erase. ⌚🎙️

**Best platform:** Facebook
**Color palette:** Antique gold, worn leather brown, cream, amber, sepia

## Concept 8: Where Memories Bloom

**Scene:** A delicate wire sculpture in the shape of a human ear sits among wildflowers in a sun-drenched garden. From the center of the ear, thin copper wires extend outward and curl into tiny flower shapes, each with a soft glowing bud at its center — representing stories blooming. The background is a soft-focus garden with dappled sunlight, dewdrops on leaves, and warm earth tones.

**Overlay text:** Every story planted. Every voice heard.

**Caption:** Like a garden, memories grow when you tend to them. Let's plant yours. 🌸🎧

**Best platform:** Instagram
**Color palette:** Copper wire, warm earth brown, sage green, soft lavender, sunlit gold

## Concept 9: The Catalog of Lives

**Scene:** A vintage wooden card catalog cabinet with many small drawers. One drawer is pulled open, and instead of index cards, tiny audio cassettes with handwritten labels nestle inside — "Dad's Fishing Stories," "Mama's Lullabies," "Trip to the Coast 1978." The drawer's metal label holder reads "HEARD AGAIN" in embossed letters. Soft library lamplight creates a quiet, intimate mood.

**Overlay text:** The library of a life. Finally organized.

**Caption:** Your family's greatest stories, curated like a library. Check out a memory today. 📚🎤

**Best platform:** Pinterest
**Color palette:** Dark wood brown, cream paper, faded label white, warm lamp glow

---

## Concept 10: Play It Once More

**Scene:** A vintage phonograph with a flared brass horn sits on an antique side table. Instead of a vinyl record, a translucent disc glows with a soft violet-purple light, and a luminous audio waveform spirals around the horn like an aura. A single violet is tucked into the brass rim. The room is dim except for the warm purple glow emanating from the phonograph, casting long shadows.

**Overlay text:** The encore their voice deserves.

**Caption:** Some performances deserve a second act. Give every voice its encore. 📀💜

**Best platform:** Instagram
**Color palette:** Brass gold, deep violet (#9C69AC), warm shadow black, cream, amber accent
