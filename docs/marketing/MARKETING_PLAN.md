# Heard Again — Marketing Plan

## Audience & Positioning

**Audience:** Music creators, artists, podcasters, audio producers looking for a creative app/platform that respects their craft and privacy.

**Key sensitivity:** The AI industry is under intense scrutiny. Many creatives are skeptical of "AI-powered" products. Our strategy is to lead with *human creativity enabled by technology* — not "AI-generated content." All marketing materials use real human photography and original design first, with AI-assisted creation as a behind-the-scenes tool.

**Tone:** Warm, creative, respectful, professional. Think "analog studio with modern tools" — not "tech startup."

**Brand colors:** Purple #9C69AC, deep teal #3F6271, sage #8EC384, warm gold #D4B68E, cream #E8D5B7
**Fonts:** Newsreader (serif headings), Manrope (sans-serif body)
